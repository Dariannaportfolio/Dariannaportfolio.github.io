Holiday Foods:


Tamales are quite simple to make. These are the ingredients and steps needed to make tamales.

Ingredients

  • Masa Dough
  • Salt
  • Corn Husks
  • Filling (Any filling of your liking, tamales are very versatile. Ex: pork, chicken, cheese, beans)

Steps

  • Soak corn husks in water for about 30 minutes
  • When dry grab a corn husk and stuff it with the filling of your choice
  • Roll, fold, and tie
  • Steam them on a stovetop for 30 mins or 20 mins in a pressure cooker

Pumpkin Pie:

Pumpkin Pie is a tradtinol fall dessert and usally made on Thanksgiving in most homes in the U.S. There are many differnt recipes for it. It usally depends on you and which recipe you like more. You can use the following recipe to make it:

  • Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • 2 Eggs Beaten
  • 1/2 TSP Salt
  • 1 can of Pumpkin
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3/4 cups of sugar
  • Pie Crust

After the mixture is ready Pour it into pie crust and bake it at 425 degrees for 15 mins.
Then lower tempture to 350 degrees and let it bake for 40-50 mins max.
